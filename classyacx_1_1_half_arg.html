Detailed Description

Class representing an half-array-argument for the kernel.
The passed float-arguments will be converted to halfs. Note the possibility of accuracy loss while conversion to half.
Required CUDA-device with CUDA version 6 or higher.
Note: The size in bytes for one half-element is fixed and may differ from the size of the corresponding data-type in CUDA, which is depending on your system. So make sure the size of the corresponding data-type is matching SIZE_BYTES to avoid unexpected segmentation faults.
